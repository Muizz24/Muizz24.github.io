Chatbot

Developed a Chatbot for DFI to respond to the FAQ of clients and to engage in conversation. Bot parses and learns messages sent to it through IBM Watson and returns a proper reply. If a response cannot be understood, users with access to the chatbot can check and give the chatbot answers to learn. This chatbot was designed with communication through DFI client's needs where we developed a storyboard and specific user requirements out of their requests. We then planned our workflow on JIRA with our team of 5 software engineers and finished the application in 3 months.
